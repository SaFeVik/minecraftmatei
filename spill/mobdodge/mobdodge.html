How to play

Controls

Avoid incoming mobs from hitting steve! Use the arrows buttons bellow to manoeuvre steve around the playing field. If you're playing on a computer, you may want to use the arrow keys for a more fluent experince.

Difficulty modes

Adjust the difficulty to compensate for you're skill level. On easy mode the mobs move at a pleasent pace, whilst on hard mode, the mobs move twice as fast for a more excilerating experience.
